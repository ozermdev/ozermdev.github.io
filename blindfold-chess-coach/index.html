Privacy Policy

Blindfold Chess Coach

Overview

Blindfold Chess Coach is designed to work fully on your device. We do not require account creation, we do not send your personal data to remote servers, and we do not monetize your data.

Summary: no user account, no cloud sync, no analytics tracking, and no ad profiling.

What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or address.
  • We do not create user accounts.
  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not collect payment details.
  • We do not collect app usage analytics.
  • We do not show ads and do not use ad-tracking SDKs.
  • We do not send gameplay or progress data to external servers.

Data Stored Locally on Device

The app stores gameplay-related settings and progress only on your device using SharedPreferences. This local data may include stage progress, settings, and feature state needed for app functionality.

This data is not transmitted to us. If you uninstall the app or clear app storage, this local data may be removed.

Third Party Services

Blindfold Chess Coach does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

The app uses flutter_local_notifications only to schedule optional reminder notifications on your device. Notification permission is requested optionally and can be declined.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users. Because this app operates locally without accounts or server-side data collection, no personal profile is created by us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product or legal changes. When updated, the revised version will replace the previous one and include an updated effective date.
